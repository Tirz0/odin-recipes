Bolonjez

Description

Fast & easy bolognese recipe

Ingredients

  • Minced meat
  • Pasta
  • Onion
  • Spices
  • Tomato sauce
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Dice the onion
  2. Put oil in a pan or a pot
  3. Fry the onion until it turns goldenish
  4. Add the meat in the pan
  5. Add spices
  6. Sear the meat until it turns light brown
  7. Let the water evaporate from the pan
  8. Add tomato sauce and let the meat cook
  9. While you cook the meat, put water in a pot
  10. When the water boils, add the pasta
  11. When it is all cooked, mix it together
  12. All done, enjoy your meal
