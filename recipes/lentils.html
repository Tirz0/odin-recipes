Lentils Recipe

Description

Quick and delicious Lentils recipe

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • Spices
  • Pancetta
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Potato (optional)
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Dice the onion and the carrot
  2. Put oil in a pot
  3. Put the onion and the carrot in the oil
  4. Fry them until the onion turns gold
  5. Add water and lentils
  6. Add spices
  7. Cook for ~30min
  8. In the meantime cut pancetta into small pieces
  9. Fry pancetta for a couple of minutes
  10. Put pancetta into the pot near the end of cooking lentils
